# Instagram-Reach-Analysis-Dashboard-Using-PowerBI
1. Project Title

Instagram Reach Analysis Dashboard using Power BI

2. Problem Statement

Social media creators and businesses often struggle to understand which content drives reach, engagement, and profile growth due to scattered Instagram metrics. Raw data from Instagram Insights is difficult to interpret without proper visualization, making it hard to identify trends, optimize posting strategies, and improve audience engagement.

3. Purpose

The purpose of this project is to:

Analyze Instagram performance metrics such as reach, impressions, likes, saves, shares, follows, and profile visits

Identify month-wise engagement trends

Provide a data-driven dashboard that helps creators and marketers make informed content decisions

Convert raw Instagram data into meaningful business insights

July recorded the highest engagement, contributing:

~53% of total follows

~35% of total saves

~40% of profile visits

Consistent growth observed from April to July

Saves and profile visits showed a strong correlation with impressions

High impressions directly translated into increased follows and engagement
